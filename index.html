"I integrate mechanical design, electronics, and control systems to bring intelligent machines to life. I build hardware that moves, flies, and navigates the physical world."

I am currently pursuing my B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering at RGUKT Basar (Class of 2027), focusing on the intersection of structural design and smart control systems. My goal is to build robots that solve real-world mobility challenges, balancing solid mechanical structures with intelligent microcontrollers and sensor integration. I thrive on translating theoretical designs into fully functional physical prototypes.

During my 5-month internship at Kinetron Technologies (Nov 2025 – Mar 2026), I worked hands-on to prototype and validate advanced tactical UAV platforms. I engineered complex mechanical transition linkages, designed waterproof enclosures for electronics, and integrated surveillance payloads.
